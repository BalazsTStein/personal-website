About

I started working as a Psychodrama Practitioner and Mental Health Professional in 2004 in Hungary. I worked with adolescents and adults with emotional and/or behavioural difficulties, providing school-based counselling, one-to-one, and group sessions. My areas of focus included mental health issues such as depression, self-harm, eating disorders, ADHD, PTSD, and more.

After moving to the UK in 2009, I continued to consolidate and extend my knowledge and skills. In 2011, I became a Registered Psychotherapist with the European Association of Psychotherapy (EAP) and was awarded the European Certificate of Psychotherapy (ECP). I arranged my registration with BPA in 2017 and with UKCP in 2018.

Over the past 18 years, I have gained experience working with a diverse range of clients. Currently, I work for a weight management company, running psychology sessions for overweight individuals. In my free time, I enjoy sports activities, competitive wrestling, and running.
